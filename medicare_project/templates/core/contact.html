Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule an appointment in several ways:

  • Call our appointments line at (123) 456-7891
  • Use our online appointment booking system
  • Email us at appointments@medicare.com
  • Visit our reception desk in person

For new patients, we recommend calling directly so we can properly assess your needs and direct you to the appropriate specialist.

Medicare accepts most major insurance plans, including:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare and Medicaid

For specific questions about your insurance coverage, please contact our billing department at (123) 456-7893.

Whether you need a referral depends on your insurance plan. Many insurance providers require a referral from your primary care physician before seeing a specialist.

We recommend checking with your insurance provider about their referral policy. If you're unsure, our staff can help guide you through the process when you contact us to schedule an appointment.

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Valid photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  • Insurance card
  • List of current medications
  • Medical records or relevant test results
  • Referral form (if required by your insurance)
  • Payment method for copays or deductibles

Arriving 15 minutes early will allow time to complete any necessary paperwork.

Medicare provides a secure patient portal where you can access your medical records, test results, and communicate with your healthcare team.

To request full medical records, please complete a medical record release form available at our reception desk or downloadable from our website. You can submit the completed form in person, by mail, or by fax to (123) 456-7899.

Please note that record requests may take 5-7 business days to process.

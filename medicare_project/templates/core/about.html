Our Story

A journey of healing, innovation, and compassionate care since 1995

Our Journey Meet Our Team

Committed to Excellence in Healthcare

For nearly three decades, Medicare has been at the forefront of healthcare excellence, driven by our unwavering commitment to patient-centered care.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services to our community while treating each patient with dignity, respect, and compassion.

Today, Medicare has grown into one of the region's most trusted healthcare providers, offering comprehensive medical services across multiple specialties. Our team of experienced healthcare professionals works tirelessly to uphold the highest standards of medical practice and patient care.

Our Journey Through the Years

1995

The Beginning

Medicare was founded by Dr. James Roberts with a small clinic and a team of 5 dedicated healthcare professionals.

2000

Expansion of Services

Expanded to include specialized departments in Cardiology, Neurology, and Pediatrics, serving over 10,000 patients annually.

2008

New Hospital Building

Moved to our current state-of-the-art facility with 200 beds and advanced medical equipment.

2015

Research & Innovation

Established our Research & Development department, focusing on innovative medical treatments and technologies.

2020

Pandemic Response

Led the community response to the global pandemic, treating over 5,000 COVID-19 patients with a recovery rate of 97%.

2025

Today & Beyond

Continuing our mission with over 500 healthcare professionals, serving more than 100,000 patients yearly, and looking forward to future innovations in patient care.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We treat every patient with kindness, empathy, and respect, understanding that healthcare is not just about treating illness but caring for people.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from medical procedures to patient interactions, constantly improving our skills and services.

Teamwork

We believe in the power of collaboration, working together across disciplines to provide the best possible care for our patients.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas, technologies, and approaches to medicine, constantly seeking better ways to diagnose, treat, and prevent illness.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards, ensuring honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions and decisions.

Community

We are committed to serving our community, addressing healthcare disparities, and improving public health through education and outreach.
